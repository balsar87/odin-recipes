Cheesy Taco Soup

Description

This is a 5 Ingredients cheesy soup that gets on your dinner table in no time. I like to serve it with tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can pinto beans, undrained
  • 1 jar black bean and corn salsa
  • 3/4 cup prepared salsa dip
  • 1 1/2 cups water

Steps

  1. Crumble ground beef in a soup pot over medium-high heat.Add taco seasoning packet
  2. Drain grease and add beef mixture back to the pot.
  3. Bring to a boil.Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes. Ladle into bowls and top with toppings of choice
