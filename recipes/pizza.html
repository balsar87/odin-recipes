Pizza

Description

This is an easy homeade pizza crust recipe that's great for beginners.

Ingredients for Pizza crust

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread flour
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps for making the

  1. Gather all ingedients.
  2. Place warm water in a bowl;add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy
  3. Add flour,oil, and salt to the yeast mixture.
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes
  5. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and roll into a circle.
  6. Put in the wanted toppings
  7. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, for 15 to 20 minutes
